I am a physicist from Guatemala. I obtained my 5-year Licentiate degree in Physics from the [School of Physical Sciences and Mathematics](https://ecfm.usac.edu.gt/) of Universidad de San Carlos de Guatemala (USAC). Following my graduation, I moved to Mexico City to pursue a Master's degree in Physics at Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM). Currently, I am a Physics PhD Candidate at UNAM---having officially advanced to candidacy after passing my qualifying exam in January 2026---working in [Carlos Pineda's group](https://gioc.fisica.unam.mx/).
